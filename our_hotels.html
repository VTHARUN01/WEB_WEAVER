OUR HOTELS
Welcome back to FUSION! We’re delighted to welcome you! You know the drill – kick off your shoes, grab a cold drink from the fridge and relax. ( we’ve stocked some waters, juice for the kids and beers for you.) Make yourself familiar with Fusion Hotels, we want to make sure you enjoy it as much as you can. We hope you feel at home here. Fusion Hotels has been in our family for three generations – and it’s been through a lot. Countless board game nights, seafood feasts on the picnic table, s’mores at the fire pit, barefoot walks along the beach, and firefly chases have happened here. We love to see all of the folks from around the world calling this house “home,” even if only for a few nights. We hope you and your extended family have a wonderful time.
